Weekend Outing Rules

  • Weekend passes must be submitted at least 24 hours prior to checkout time.
  • Outing permissions are granted only after receiving automated SMS confirmation from parents.
  • Late entries beyond 08:00 PM on Sunday will attract disciplinary action.
  • Students must carry their college ID card and biometric punch is mandatory.

Holiday/Vacation Guidelines

  • Holiday requests must be raised 3 days in advance and require Warden approval.
  • In case of medical leaves, students must produce a medical certificate during checkin.
  • Mess charges rebated only if the student is absent for 5 days or more with prior approval.
  • Students must clean rooms and shut off all electrical appliances before leaving.
